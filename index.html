Discover more about me.

Hello, my name is Salem Gonzalez. I’m from Mexico. I arrived in the U.S.A when I was 14 years old. When I came to this country I found it challenging, I was seeing things differently such as; school, friends, language, food etc... My parents always taught me that if I wanted to be a successful man I needed to work hard, be honest, and humble with anything I do or say. One of the most important things was to learn to speak the language, read it, understand it, and comprehend it in order to communicate with people.

My biggest challenge in this country was to speak the language. Being born in a Spanish speaking country and then moving to the U.S.A in just a matter of days, it was difficult for me and I’m sure it was for many as well. Learning and speaking the language has given me so many great opportunities, so many open doors on things like, places and people I didn’t imagine to see or meet in which I thank God for.

Photography is a challenge. There is always something new to learn every day. Taking this challenge there is no going back. I used to do it as a hobby because I liked it, but now it's grown in me in a very inexplicable way and I’m treating it seriously and professionally hoping to meet new people, places and be part of their special moments and share memories through a photo. I enjoy seeing people happy with my work that keeps me motivated to strive to become a better photographer.

Thank you for your time. I will be happy to assist you and make business with you.
